Basic hand tools. Your driveway. Under an hour. Our bolt-on system is engineered so that any diesel owner can install it without special equipment or dealer visits.

1 Step One

Remove the Stock Pipe

Loosen the factory couplers at the turbocharger outlet and intercooler inlet. The OEM pipe slides straight out. No cutting. No specialty tools. 10 minutes.
