Mobile Performance: 28 — Critical

A score of 28 means the site fails on nearly every mobile metric. Mobile users on 4G experience a 7.2s LCP and 3,120ms of JavaScript blocking — more than 3 full seconds where the page appears frozen. Over 60% of web traffic is mobile.

Desktop Performance: 77 — Needs Work

Desktop performs significantly better but still has room to improve, particularly LCP (2.2s) and TBT (200ms). Image delivery and cache savings of 2,858 KiB remain unaddressed on both devices.

Summary

The site scores 28/100 on mobile and 77/100 on desktop — a 49-point gap that reflects heavy JavaScript execution (10 seconds of main-thread work on mobile), unoptimised images, and 35 third-party script domains. Google's Core Web Vitals assessment is currently failing due to a CLS of 0.27, which is directly traceable to missing image dimensions and a broken carousel. The first 7 actions in this plan are low-effort quick wins that alone are expected to move the mobile score from 28 to 45+, resolve the CWV failure, and improve both SEO and Accessibility scores.
