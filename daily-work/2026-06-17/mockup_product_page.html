Sinister Diesel Fuel Filter Conversion Kit — 6.7L Powerstroke (2011–2026)

The factory 6.7L Powerstroke fuel filter housing is one of the most common failure points on these trucks. Over time, the factory plastic housing becomes prone to cracking, warping, and leaking from repeated heat cycles, vibration, and fuel system service. The Sinister Diesel Fuel Filter Conversion Kit permanently eliminates these factory weak points with a precision CNC-machined billet aluminium housing.

Key Features

  • Billet Aluminium Fuel Filter Housing — permanently replaces the failure-prone factory plastic housing
  • True 2011–2026 Compatibility — includes integrated third fuel port for 2017+ trucks plus threaded plug for 2011–2016 applications
  • Updated Leak-Resistant Design — redesigned housing eliminates unnecessary side ports
  • Improved OEM Quick Connect Ports — redesigned fittings allow easier installation of factory fuel line connections
  • Grooved O-Ring Sealing System — improved sealing design helps simplify installation while reducing seepage risk
  • Baldwin 4-Micron Nominal Filter — matches OEM-level filtration standards (9µm OEM vs our 4µm)
  • CNC-Machined Billet Construction — built to handle heat, pressure, vibration, and long-term service
  • Direct Bolt-On Installation — no cutting, no fabrication, no extra adapters required

Common Problems This Solves

  • Cracked factory plastic fuel filter housing
  • Fuel leaks at the filter housing or cap
  • Diesel fuel spray in the engine bay
  • Weak factory plastic construction
  • Leak-prone multi-adapter competitor kits
  • Difficult-to-service retention sleeve systems

What's Included

  • CNC-Machined Billet Aluminium Fuel Filter Housing
  • Baldwin BF7967 4-Micron Nominal Fuel Filter
  • Integrated 3rd Port with Threaded Plug (2011–2016)
  • Billet Aluminium Fuel Fittings
  • Complete Installation Hardware
