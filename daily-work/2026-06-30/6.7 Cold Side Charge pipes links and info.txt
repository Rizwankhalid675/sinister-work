Product Overview
The factory cold-side charge pipe on the 2011–2016 6.7L Powerstroke is plastic. Over time, heat cycles, vibration, and boost pressure cause the plastic pipe to weaken, expand, or crack — especially under towing or tuned conditions.

Plastic expands under boost. Aluminum does not.

Sinister Diesel replaces the failure-prone factory cold-side pipe with a full 3" mandrel-bent, TIG-welded aluminum charge pipe engineered for strength and airflow efficiency.

Independent airflow testing confirms up to 27% less restriction compared to factory piping across the usable airflow range.

Lower restriction improves charge system efficiency and helps maintain consistent boost delivery under load.

Why 3" Instead of 3.5"? Some aftermarket options advertise oversized 3.5" tubing. While larger diameter may sound appealing, airflow performance is not determined by size alone.

Charge air systems must balance diameter, air velocity, and turbo response. Oversizing tubing beyond what the turbo and intercooler can efficiently support can reduce air velocity and increase system volume without delivering measurable gains.

Sinister Diesel engineered this cold-side pipe at 3" to optimize airflow efficiency, maintain proper air velocity, and support real-world towing and tuned applications — without sacrificing boost response.

This diameter provides substantial airflow improvement over the restrictive factory plastic pipe while preserving system balance and drivability.

More aluminum means more strength.

Some aftermarket kits rely heavily on silicone sections instead of full aluminum construction. Silicone can expand and billow under boost, affecting airflow consistency. Aluminum maintains its shape under pressure, preserving clean airflow and improving long-term durability.

To further prevent boost leaks, our silicone boot features a molded No-Slip Lip — an internal ridge that mechanically locks onto the pipe bead. Combined with our 3/4" wide stainless steel T-bolt clamps, this design helps prevent boot slippage under heavy boost or tuned applications.

Unlike many competitors, Sinister Diesel includes new O-rings and retaining clips. Many upgrades require reusing factory sealing components that have been heat-cycled and fatigued.

This is not a cosmetic upgrade. It is a structural and airflow improvement designed to eliminate weak points in the charge system.

Cold Side Charge Pipe for 2011–2016 Ford Powerstroke 6.7L (Gray)
Eliminate a known weak point on your 2011–2016 Ford Powerstroke 6.7L with the Sinister Diesel Cold Side Charge Pipe. The factory plastic pipe is prone to cracking or blowing apart under boost. Our upgrade uses 6061 aluminum and CNC mandrel-bent 3" tubing for smooth, reliable airflow and quicker turbo response.

Includes new O-ring and locking ring for a factory-tight seal, plus 5-ply silicone boots and stainless spring clamps. Finished in Sinister Gray powder coat. 50-State Legal under CARB EO D-721-12. Backed by a 2-Year Warranty.

Key Features & Benefits
- Direct OEM Replacement for failure-prone factory plastic pipe
- 6061 Aluminum Construction — lightweight and built for high boost
- CNC Mandrel-Bent 3" Tubing for smooth, unrestricted airflow
- 5-Ply Silicone Boots (rated to ~400 PSI) & Stainless Spring Clamps
- Powder Coated Gray for corrosion resistance and a clean look
- 50-State Legal (CARB EO D-721-12)
- 2-Year Warranty

Allow the engine to cool. Remove the factory cold side pipe and retainers. Install the Sinister Diesel pipe with the new O-ring and locking ring, then fit the 5-ply boots and stainless spring clamps. Verify all connections, start the engine, and check for boost leaks.

Product Overview
Maximize the performance and efficiency of your 2017+ Ford 6.7L PowerStroke V-8 with the Sinister Diesel Cold Side Charge Pipe. This upgraded charge pipe replaces the restrictive factory piping that connects the intercooler to your engine. With a larger 3-inch diameter and smooth mandrel bends, the cold side charge pipe increases airflow velocity, boosting engine power and reducing EGTs for improved overall performance.

Built from 6061 aluminum alloy, the Sinister Diesel Cold Side Charge Pipe offers superior durability and reliability. Paired with 5-ply silicone boots rated to 400psi and stainless steel spring clamps, this kit ensures a secure, leak-free installation. Each pipe is TIG welded and finished in Limited Edition Sinister Gray for added corrosion resistance and a polished, professional appearance. Patent pending and designed for maximum performance, this upgrade is perfect for diesel enthusiasts who demand quality and results.

Please note: This product is currently not compatible with the 2024 Cab & Chassis Truck equipped with the additional sensor. However, it fits perfectly with the 2024 Pickup model. Designed for reliability and efficiency, this upgrade ensures consistent results for daily driving, towing, or heavy-duty applications.
